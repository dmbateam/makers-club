refund policy

last updated: 22 april 2026

cancel anytime

ai makers club is a month-to-month subscription. you can cancel any time from your paddle account or by emailing us at support@d.mba. cancellation stops future renewals — you keep access until the end of the period you've already paid for.

no pro-rata refunds

we do not refund the unused portion of the current billing month when you cancel. if you cancel mid-month, your access continues to the end of that month and no further charges are made.

billing errors & faulty service

if you were charged in error (e.g. a duplicate charge, a failed cancellation, or a bug on our side), email support@d.mba within 30 days of the charge and we will refund it.

statutory rights

if you live in the eu/uk and you bought as a consumer, you have a 14-day statutory right of withdrawal from the start of your subscription. the membership grants you immediate access to digital content (the weekly brief and community), so by purchasing you agree the service begins immediately and that the 14-day right of withdrawal does not apply once the first brief has been made available to you. if you have not yet accessed any content, you can request a refund within 14 days at the email above.
