01 — the rhythm

one week, three beats.

7 days · mon → sun
01

monday · drop

a new brief lands at 09:00 GMT. one page. one specific challenge. with just enough guidance so you can get going. 

02

the week · build

you pick your stack, scope it to something shippable, and make. if you get stuck, get help by club members.

03

sunday · ship

post what you made by 24:00 GMT. review what others have made. teach, learn, and connect. 

02 — the manifesto

one club.
five "rules".

five rules. read them aloud the first monday of every month.

  1. you do not talk about the club.

  2. bring what you know. borrow what you don't.

  3. ship beats polish. sunday beats someday.

  4. make the thing you wanted to exist on monday morning.

  5. see rule number 01.

apply to join the room.

we read every one. if it's a fit, you'll hear from us before the next monday drop.
