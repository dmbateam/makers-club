privacy notice

last updated: 22 april 2026

who controls your data

d.MBA d.o.o., a company registered in slovenia, is the data controller for personal data collected through ai makers club. contact: hello@joinaimakers.club.

what we collect

  • account data: your email address and name, given when you join.
  • payment data: handled entirely by paddle. we receive transaction metadata (amount, date, country) but never see your full card details.
  • community activity: posts, comments, and profile data inside our private circle community.

why we process it

  • to give you access to the membership and weekly briefs (contractual necessity).
  • to email you about your membership and the briefs (contractual necessity / legitimate interest).
  • to comply with our tax and accounting obligations (legal obligation).

who we share it with

we use the following processors. each handles your data only for the purpose stated and on our instructions:

  • paddle — payment processing, billing, tax compliance (merchant of record).
  • circle — hosting the private community.
  • kit — sending the weekly brief and member emails.
  • google workspace — internal email, files, and operations.

cookies & tracking

this website does not set tracking cookies and does not use analytics. paddle and circle may set functional cookies inside their own checkout and community surfaces, governed by their own notices.

your rights (gdpr)

if you live in the eu/uk you have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data, and to object to or restrict its processing. email hello@joinaimakers.club and we will respond within 30 days. you also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

retention

we keep your account data while you are a member and for up to 24 months after cancellation, then delete it. invoices and payment records are kept longer where required by tax law.

changes

material changes will be announced in the community before they take effect.
